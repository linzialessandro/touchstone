# Career bet (aligned with Touchstone)

Hireable **research engineer** in **reasoning & evaluation**, by shipping **Touchstone**: a public **AI coding-reasoning** benchmark on Kaggle.

## Honest product goal

| Goal | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Topic** | AI coding / reasoning benchmarks |
| **Venue** | Kaggle (Community Benchmarks + dataset; free GPU for open models) |
| **Grok’s “win”** | **Benchmark designer** — Touchstone is good enough to matter |
| **Who competes** | **Open-weight models only** on the leaderboard |

Not: Grok 4.5 vs Qwen on the same LB.  
Yes: Grok helps design the arena; open models race; success = design credibility.

## Product identity

| In | Out |
|----|-----|
| Open-only competitive LB | Closed models as official contestants |
| Fair executable metric | Rigged difficulty for a brand narrative |
| Designer credit for Grok | “Grok model score #1” as the win condition |
| Kaggle free GPU for open field | Paid API required to participate |

## Success signals

1. Live dataset + Benchmark with clear open-only rules.  
2. ≥3 open models scored on Kaggle GPU.  
3. One-liner holds: *designed with Grok; open models compete*.  
4. You can defend metric and contamination in an RE interview.
